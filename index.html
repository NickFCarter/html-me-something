The Thrilling Life of a Television Celebrity

In the last half of 2017, I made four nationally broadcast television appearances. Which means I'm basically famous.

TV Debut: August 2017

I made my TV debut when I skyped into the season premiere of The Chris Gethard Show on TruTV

A Visit From the Big City Talk Show Host

After my call, the host of the show, Chris Gethard, traveled overnight from NYC to Affton to deliver prizes to my apartment. The video of his visit was shown on the next week's episode

Conan

The next week, Gethard appeared on Conan to promote the new season of the show, and he brought along the video of his visit to my apartment.

Road Trip

A few months later, I drove all the way to New York to attend the season finale in person.

The crew at the show gave me the best seat in the house, right by all the action!
